# Risk Factors

Participants should carefully consider the following risks before acquiring 12C tokens.

##  Market Risks
*   **Volatility:** Token prices may fluctuate significantly due to market sentiment.
*   **Liquidity:** There is no guarantee of active trading markets or liquidity.
*   **Competition:** The project faces competition from other blockchain and cultural platforms.

##  Technical Risks
*   **Smart Contract Bugs:** Despite audits, code vulnerabilities may exist.
*   **Blockchain Dependency:** We rely on the stability of Arbitrum and Ethereum.
*   **Security Breaches:** Hacking, phishing, and wallet theft are ongoing threats.

##  Regulatory Risks
*   **Legal Uncertainty:** Regulations regarding cryptocurrencies vary by jurisdiction and may change.
*   **Classification:** Tokens could be classified as securities in the future, affecting tradability.

*Note: This list is not exhaustive. Participants assume all risks associated with the project.*
